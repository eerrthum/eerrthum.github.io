Course Objectives:     Increase your conceptual understanding of mathematics. See how other students solve problems. Understand when your answer is right – and why it is right. Work with other students to solve challenging and fun problems.

What this course is NOT:       A place to work on regular course homework. Additional instruction outside of your regular Math 120 course.

Grading:     The grade in this course is based solely on your attendance and participation. To be counted present you need to be in the classroom within 5 minutes of the start of class and leave no earlier than 5 minutes before the end of class. If you are tardy or have to leave early, it will count as half of a miss. If you miss the whole period, it will count as a whole miss. (2 half misses = 1 whole miss). Attendance taking will start on Monday, September 8. Participation will be based on your class reflections. Attending class but failing to hand in a completed reflection will result in half of a miss.

Grading Scale:            0 – 5.5 misses     Pass
                                    ≥ 6 misses           No Pass
