The Informal Meetings of the
Winona State Problem Solvers

 

Fridays: 3-4pm in the MAC (GI135)

All are welcome, all are invited.

 

What we’ll be doing:
We’ll all sit around looking at some really hard problem sets from
American Mathematical Monthly, College Mathematics Journal, or Mathematics Magazine.
We may never arrive at the solutions, but, if we do, the goal would be
to write and submit the solution(s) for publication.

Things you might want to bring:
Paper, pencil, calculator, laptop (with Mathematica, Matlab, or Maple)
