MATH 440: Chapter 17 Write-Up Problems\vspace{1em}

Name:\vspace{1em}\\

Recall: When it's a disproof, you get an addition 2 bonus points for stating a true statement and proving it.\\

\begin{enumerate}

\item Let $F$ be a field and let $$I = \{a_nx^n+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+\cdots+a_0 \mid a_i \in F \text{ and } a_n+a_{n-1}+\cdots+a_0 = 0\}.$$ Show that $I$ is an ideal of $F[x]$. By Theorem 17.12, it is principal, so also give the generator.
\item In the ring $\mathbb{R}[x]/\langle x^2+7x+2\rangle$, compute the multiplicative inverse of $3x+1$. In other words find $(3x+1)^{-1} \mod x^2+7x+2$.
\item Prove or disprove: The ideal $\langle x \rangle$ in $\mathbb{Q}[x]$ is maximal.

\end{enumerate}
