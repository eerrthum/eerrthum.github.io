MATH 440: Chapter 5 Write-Up Problems\vspace{1em}

Name:\vspace{1em}
\begin{enumerate}

\item Let $\sigma=(a\ b\ c)\in S_4$. Prove or disprove: For all $K \subseteq S_4$, $\sigma K=K \sigma$.


\item  Let $\sigma$ be the $m$-cycle $(1\ 2\ 3\ \dots\ m)$. Show that  if $\sigma^i$ is also a $m$-cycle, then $\gcd(m,i)=1$.

\item Let $G$ be the group of permutations on a set $X$. Let $a \in X$ and define the stabilizer of $a$ as $$\text{stab}(a)=\{g \in G \mid g(a)=a\}.$$ Prove or disprove: $\text{stab}(a)$ is a subgroup of $G$.

\end{enumerate}
