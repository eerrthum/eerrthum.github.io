MATH 440: Chapter 16 Write-Up Problems\vspace{1em}

Name:\vspace{1em}\\

Recall: When it's a disproof, you get an addition 2 bonus points for stating a true statement and proving it.\\

\begin{enumerate}

\item Prove or disprove: Let $R$ be a ring with unity $1_R$ and $S$ a nontrivial subring of $R$ with unity $1_S$. Prove or disprove that $1_R=1_S$.
\item Prove or disprove: If $1_R=0_R$ then $R=\{0\}$.
\item Consider the subring of matrices $R = \left\{\begin{pmatrix}a & b \\ 2b & a\end{pmatrix}\mid a,b \in \mathbb{Z}\right\}$. Show that $R$ is an integral domain. (You can assume that $R$ is a ring with unity.)

\end{enumerate}
