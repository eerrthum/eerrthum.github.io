How To: Apply for a WSU Math/Stats Scholarship for the 2010-2011 Academic Year

 

NOTE: The following instructions are the minimal requirements for applying to scholarships offered in the Math/Stat Department. However, it is in your best interest to complete the application as entirely as you can in order to apply for all scholarships for which you are eligible across campus. If you have any questions, please contact Brant Deppa or Eric Errthum.

 

Step 1:

·        Go to the url: http://wsufoundation.scholarships.ngwebsolutions.com/

Step 2:

·        Log in if you have already used this website to apply for other WSU scholarships. If you are new to this website, follow the directions for creating a login.

Step 3:

·        Click “Start Application” (or “Continue Application” if you already started one for another scholarship on campus).

Step 4:

·        Fill in all the required fields in the “Student Demographics” dialog box on the first page.

Step 5:

·        Answer the above four questions on the second page in the “Student Information” dialog box. (For MathEd the question about “officially been accepted into your program” refers to your admittance into the College of Education. For Math and Stat majors, you may leave this blank.)

Step 6:

·        On the third page, answer the above question in the “Additional Information” dialog box.

Step 7:

·        In the “Clubs” dialog box, check Math/Stat club if appropriate.

Step 8:

·        On the fourth page, respond to the general essay about Goals and Objectives. You should specifically mention how your Math, MathEd, or Stat degree fits into these goals and objectives.

Step 9:

·        If you are an international student who has financial need but has not filled out a FAFSA form, please complete the “International Students” dialog box also on the fourth page.

Step 10:

·        On the fifth page, in the “Letter of Recommendation” dialog box, make sure to enter at least one faculty member from the Math/Stat Department.

Step 11:

·        Click “Save and Continue to Summary Page”. From here you’ll be able to review your scholarship application and submit.
